Amaravati

The Vision of Amaravati

Amaravati, envisioned as the capital of Andhra Pradesh, represents a modern and sustainable urban development model. Located on the banks of the Krishna River, the city is designed to blend rich cultural heritage with futuristic infrastructure.

🏛️ Cultural Heritage

Amaravati has deep historical roots, known for its ancient Buddhist sites and rich traditions.

🌆 Smart City Vision

The city is planned with smart infrastructure, green spaces, and sustainable mobility.

🌱 Sustainable Growth

Focus on eco-friendly development with riverfront planning and green energy solutions.
